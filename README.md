# Bodyweight WorkoutRandomizer
A flexible xOS bodyweight workout app that generates randomized exercise routines based on your focus areas and difficulty, with guided video demos, an Apple Watch companion, and HealthKit integration.

## Features

- Random Routine Generator - Select your focus areas and difficulty level and let the app build a workout for you
- Focus Area Selection - Target Chest, Legs, Shoulders, Triceps, Glutes, Core, Cardio, or any combination
- Difficulty Levels - Choose from Beginner, Medium, Hard, or Expert/Advanced
- Configurable Workout Settings - Set total duration, per-exercise duration, rest duration, and how often to rest
- Exercise Demo Videos - Watch guided video demonstrations for each exercise, with stream or download-to-device options
- Auto-Updating Exercise Catalog - New exercises and videos are picked up automatically without an app update
- Timer Controls - Pause, resume, skip, or cancel your routine at any time
- Audio Alerts & Haptics - Get audio and haptic feedback when each exercise starts, ends, or is about to end
- Screen Wake - Keeps your device screen active throughout your workout
- Import/Export - Save and share your generated routines as JSON files
- Apple Watch Companion - See the current exercise name on your wrist and start a HealthKit workout session
- HealthKit Integration - Track heart rate and active calories from your Apple Watch in real time

## Use Cases

- Bodyweight strength training
- HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)
- Targeted muscle group workouts
- Cardio and conditioning routines
- On-the-go workouts with no equipment

## Requirements

- iOS/iPadOS 18.6 or later
- macOS 15.6 or later
- visionOS 26.2 or later
- watchOS 26.2 or later (companion app)

## Privacy
This app does not collect or transmit any personal data. All workout routines are stored locally on your device (or whatever cloud storage services you have set up on your device). Health data is only exchanged between your own paired iPhone and Apple Watch and is never sent to external servers.
